Media

A mainstream news story here appears in a somewhat different context abroad

I have not selected these examples with any particular agenda. Below is only a small sample of mostly right-wing online media from the United States, the United Kingdom, Finland, Italy, Australia, Poland, Portugal, and Spain.

Here are examples of websites that sit another level below these in terms of quality: more hate speech, more disinformation, including around issues such as climate change and vaccines.

The original source for most of the articles below is largely either Helsingin Sanomat's interview with Kaltiala or the essay published by The Free Press, "Gender-Affirming Care Is Dangerous. I Know Because I Helped Pioneer It.".

  • National Review - The transgender house of cards just came crashing down - This article from a prominent conservative publication with a long history is behind a paywall, but the visible section already talks about "pumping children full of chemicals".

  • Spiked - The dangers of gender-affirming care are now undeniable - A British gender-critical publication connects the study to puberty blockers, quotes the study, and the writer also finds room to attack the Good Law Project, which exposed an anti-trans speaking tour at British universities backed by Putin.

  • Washington Stand - Finnish Study Links Transgender Procedures to Decline in Youth Mental Health - A fundamentalist Christian site reports on the Finnish study, which received considerable media attention, largely based on Tampere University's press release. The original contribution is a comment from an "expert" at the Discovery Institute, known for rebranding "intelligent design" creationism. In other words, a person whose scientific worldview includes supernatural phenomena.

  • The Other Cheek - Finnish study shows increased psychiatric morbidity after trans treatment - An Australian Christian publication also reports on the study based on Tampere University's press release.

  • Family First NZ - New Finnish research challenges “lifesaving” claim for youth gender treatment - A New Zealand "pro-family, pro-life watchdog" publication also joins in advancing Finnish science.

  • WSJ - Finland’s Lessons on Transgender Children

  • WSJ - Will Doctors Follow the Science on Gender-Confused Kids? - The Wall Street Journal's opinion section wrote about the study twice, calling it a "scientific tour-de-force". The WSJ opinion section has long been criticized even by its own newsroom, and topics such as climate-change skepticism and the lab leak hypothesis are among the causes promoted by anti-woke WSJ Opinion. Here too, a member of the Koch dynasty appeared in the accompanying video speaking about faith and patriotism at their event.
